Anna Durai

Chennai's Most Loved AutoDriver

This Auto Rickshaw driver drives a super luxury auto on the Chennai Roads.

Anna Durai AKA ‘Auto Anna' is an inspirational TEDx speaker and keynote speaker who provides safety and satisfaction to his customers through his auto services. He strongly believes in "Atithi Devo Bavah" means "Guest is God". He treats every customer as his guest and gives them the best hospitality no autorickshaw in the entire India can provide. He is an inspiration to many entrepreneurs, students, youth and many other auto drivers in the country.

“Passengers give me my daily bread. They are Gods and I have to satisfy their needs”

-Anna Durai

Some Interesting Facts about Anna Durai

  • TED Talk speaker and Keynote speaker
  • Anna was honoured for his excellence by Arjun Ram Meghwal, Union Minister
  • Honoured with Uchitra Award by L&D Global
  • He is maintain a website in the name of amazingauto.in in 2014
  • Anna earns Rs. 45,000 per month and spends Rs. 9500 every month on his auto
  • He offers discounts and gifts to his customers by through customer relationship contests
  • He installed Samsung Galaxy Tab II Note 8 for around Rs. 30,000 in 2013 on loan
  • Currently, his customers are enjoying the experience of Apple iPad Pro which he bought for around Rs. 80,000 with his savings
"Money may come and go, but the feeling you get when your customer chooses to travel only in your auto is unmatched."

-Anna Durai
